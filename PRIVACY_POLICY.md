# Privacy Policy for ordakGov2

**Last Updated:** November 15, 2025

## Introduction

ordakGov2 ("we", "our", or "the App") is a Shopify application that provides delivery and pickup scheduling services for merchants and their customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### From Merchants

When you install and use our App, we collect:

- **Shop Information:** Your Shopify shop domain, shop name, and basic shop configuration
- **Access Token:** Encrypted OAuth token to communicate with your Shopify store
- **Configuration Data:** Settings for delivery zones, locations, time slots, and business rules
- **Order Information:** Order IDs, order numbers, and scheduling preferences selected by customers
- **Location Data:** Physical addresses and coordinates of pickup/delivery locations

### From Customers (End Users)

When customers use the delivery/pickup scheduling features:

- **Contact Information:** Email address and phone number (optional)
- **Delivery Information:** Delivery address and postcode for eligibility checking
- **Scheduling Preferences:** Selected delivery/pickup time slots and locations
- **Recommendation Data:** Anonymous interaction data to improve slot recommendations

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### For Merchants

- Provide delivery and pickup scheduling functionality
- Check postcode eligibility for delivery services
- Manage delivery zones and time slot capacity
- Generate recommendations for optimal scheduling
- Provide analytics and insights about booking patterns
- Send webhooks to external routing/dispatch systems
- Comply with app uninstall and GDPR requirements

### For Customers

- Display available delivery/pickup time slots
- Provide personalized slot recommendations
- Check delivery eligibility for postcodes
- Improve the user experience through analytics

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- **Database Security:** Customer and merchant data stored in secure PostgreSQL database
- **Access Control:** Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
- **OAuth Security:** Shopify OAuth tokens stored securely and never exposed

### Data Retention

- **Active Merchants:** Data retained while app is installed and active
- **Uninstalled Apps:** Shop data deleted within 48 hours of app uninstall
- **Customer Data:** Retained as required for fulfillment and analytics, deleted upon GDPR request

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### We DO share data with:

- **Shopify:** Required data shared via Shopify API for app functionality
- **External Routing Services:** Order scheduling data sent via signed webhooks (if configured by merchant)

### We DO NOT:

- Sell your data to third parties
- Use customer data for marketing without consent
- Share data with unauthorized third parties

## Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

### For Merchants

- **Access:** Request access to all data we store about your shop
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your shop data by uninstalling the app
- **Portability:** Export your configuration and scheduling data
- **Modification:** Update your settings and configuration at any time

### For Customers

- **Access:** Request all data we have collected about you
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Opt-out:** Opt out of data collection for recommendations

To exercise these rights, contact us at: [support@ordakgov2.com] (Update with actual email)

## GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) by:

1. **Data Minimization:** Only collecting necessary data for app functionality
2. **Purpose Limitation:** Using data only for stated purposes
3. **Storage Limitation:** Deleting data when no longer needed
4. **Transparency:** Clearly communicating data practices
5. **Individual Rights:** Honoring all GDPR data subject rights

### GDPR Webhooks

We handle the following Shopify GDPR webhooks:

- **CUSTOMERS_DATA_REQUEST:** Export all customer data upon request
- **CUSTOMERS_REDACT:** Delete all customer personal data
- **SHOP_REDACT:** Delete all shop data 48 hours after app uninstall

## Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal session-based tracking:

- **Session IDs:** Temporary session identifiers for recommendation tracking
- **No Persistent Cookies:** We do not set long-term cookies
- **No Cross-Site Tracking:** We do not track users across websites

## Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your information:

- We will notify affected parties within 72 hours
- We will provide details about the breach and remediation steps
- We will comply with all applicable data breach notification laws

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
